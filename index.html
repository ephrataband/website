Sometimes swirly, sometimes peppy, always dreamy.

Ephrata was formed in 2011 when Seattle musician Brady Hall met Skadi von Reis and they collaborated on some recordings. Jules Jones and Ben Bromage joined in to form the full band in 2012 and they played their first show in early 2013. They focus on sugary cascades of vocal harmony, lush soundscapes of shoegazey guitar, and sixties "wall of sound" power mixed with dream pop and psychedelia. In addition to playing around the Seattle area they also record their own music, make their own videos and take their own photos.
